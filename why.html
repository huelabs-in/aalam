Welcome to Aalam

What’s special about Aalam?

Aalam’s school facility is designed by architects from Harvard University.

Aalam’s activities focus on eight different types of intelligences proposed by Prof. Howard Gardner’s Theory of Multiple Intelligences.

Aalam follows a structured International Preschool curriculum designed by Ms. Prema Daniel - Director, Centre for Training and Research in Early Childhood.

An eminent Board of Advisors and Board of Parents who guides Aalam towards our vision.

Aalam’s teachers are not just facilitators of learning. But also trained and sensitised on inclusivity. Last but on least, excellent human beings.

About Aalam

Aalam learning centre is your neighbourhood, minimalistic play school focussing on creating joyous learning environment!
